Services

Condition Reports

  • Provide an in-depth assessment of the condition of an artwork or object from which care and conservation advice can be provided.
  • Establish the true nature of the condition of a piece prior to insuring an item or before a piece is loaned or shipped.
  • Assist with potential sale of an item – adding to provenance and giving buyer peace of mind when making a transaction.
  • Help to prevent long-term damage.

Conservation & Restoration

  • Assessment of an object or artwork for treatment including paintings, works on paper (watercolours, prints etc), sculpture, ceramics and glass, gilded frames, metal, furniture and modern and contemporary art.
  • Preventive measures – such as conservation cleaning or stabilisation and recommendations for storage or display, including display cases and bespoke mounts.
  • Free estimates and treatment reports.

Project Management

  • Condition reporting and assessment of collections.
  • Specialist on site cleaning.
  • Preventive treatments including environmental control and monitoring of pest and insect infestation to ensure stability of the collection.
  • Advising following insurance claims – treatment for fine art and objects suffering from fire, water and transit/handling issues.
  • Storage solutions.

Training

  • Advising household staff on maintenance and care of collection.
  • Presentation to the insurance sector on what can be achieved post disaster.
  • Bespoke packages tailored to client’s requirements.
