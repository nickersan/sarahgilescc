About

Sarah Giles Collection Care works with art, antiques and objects of all types, offering a personal and trusted approach to advise on issues such as preventive conservation, conservation and restoration.

Working with a large network of industry specialists who consistently adhere to conservation best practice, we ensure that each job is approached with sensitivity and work is completed to the highest of standards, within an agreed timescale and at a competitive price.

Sarah Giles Collection Care was founded following a 23-year career at London-based art restoration service Plowden & Smith, where Sarah worked with specialist conservators and restorers, gaining an extensive general knowledge of collection care and a network of industry experts.

Today we work with clients around the globe to help preserve collections for the long term, thus protecting their value and safeguarding pieces for future generations.

Sarah has a Joint Honours Degree in Art History & English and a Post-Graduate Qualification in Fine Art Valuation. Sarah is on the Committee for FAIR – The Fine Art Insurance Reception.
